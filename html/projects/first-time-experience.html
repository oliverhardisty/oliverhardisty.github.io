First Time Experience
project company
ClearScore
project type
First time experience for new users
user problem
A proportion of users are not discovering the core benefits of the ClearScore app on their first visit. These users are much less likely to return to the app in the future and so don’t get the apps continued benefit.
business problem
A proportion of users are not logging into ClearScore after 2 months from signing up. Research shows, these users are much less likely to sustain future engagement in the product.
context
As outlined in the user problem, a proportion of users are not visiting ClearScores core functions in their first time experience of the app. These core functions are Report (users latest credit report) and Offers (marketplace of credit products based on users credit data). Report gives users significant user value by giving them their new credit report every month for free. In light of this, the primary goal of this project is to increase the proportion of users visiting Report. Secondary goals include increasing Offers discovery and overall engagement.
our hypothesis
Providing users a first time experience that informs and directs users to Report and Offers will increase the proportion of users visiting and discovering the benefits of these functions.
Success metrics
relevant insight
Key relevant insights we’ve gathered from product analytics and research include;
- 15% of users are not visiting Report in their first time experience of the app.
- 37% of users are not visiting Offers in their first time experience of the app.
- Users who visit Report and/or Offers in their first and second months after signing up, are significantly more likely to sustain future engagement in the product.
- Personalised messages always out performs generic messaging.
