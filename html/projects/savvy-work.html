project context
Savvy is a startup setup to address the needs of commercial property managers and tennants. Savvy identified the working culture to be essential to staff satisfaction and retention. Today, office companies have a number of digital tools to improve working efficiency, however, tools addressing their social needs are limited. Savvy Work is a Proof of Concept app, designed to improve companies working culture by addressing their key social needs.
core functions
Feed - Users can see updates, posts and automated insights.
Events - Users can setup and manage communication on social events.
Offers - Users can claim offer deals from service merchants supplying the area.
Clubs - Users can setup and manage communication on social clubs.
Feedback - Users can feedback on the social culture of their company.
