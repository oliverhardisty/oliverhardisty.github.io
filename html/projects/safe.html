Safe
project type
Proof of Concept app
project company
Jigsaw XYZ
project context
Following the success of alternative banking products in Fintech, Jigsaw XYZ identified opportunity for similar innovation in the digital insurance space. Safe is a Proof of Concept app, designed to allow users take control of their insurance needs and protect their most valuable possessions.
core functions
Home - Users can see better value insurance quotes than their existing policies.
Shop - Users can shop a wide range of insurance on the market.
Cover - Users can view all their insurance details in one place.
Tips - Users can get insights on how to keep their insured possessions safe.
platforms
iOS & Android
approx. time frame
04-06.2018

Based on users existing policy details, Safe searches for better value quotes on the market. If Safe finds any, users can see and explore them on Home (centre screen).

Users can visually compare any new quote with their existing policy (right screen). Home also gives users insights on how to keep their insured possessions safe (left screen).

For Safe to find better value quotes, users must upload details of their existing policies. Once uploaded, the user can view all these details in one place, in Cover (left & centre screen).

For users who want to find new quotes themselves, users can search a wide range of insurance on the market, in Shop (right).
