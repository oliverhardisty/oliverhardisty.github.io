Switch and Save

project company

ClearScore

project type

Feature for ClearScores marketplace

user problem

Users carrying over balances on their credit cards don’t know how much they could save with a balance transfer card and so are less motivated to save money this way.

business problem

Users less likely to purchase Balance transfer cards from ClearScores marketplace.

context

A large majority of users carry over balances on their credit cards, often paying high interests on this debt. We predicted we could save around £200 million for our users, if they were to switch their credit cards to a balance transfer card. The Switch and Save project aims to inform users how much they could save and guide them through the process of switching. In light of this, the experience would have to tackle a number of design problems. For example, how do we educate users about balance transfer cards and how can we get users to trust the saving calculation.

our hypothesis

Guiding users about switching to a balance transfer card and indicating how much they could save will motivate them to switch.

success metrics

relevant insight

Key insights we’ve gathered from data calculations, previous projects and campaigns include;

- We could help save £200 million for our users
- Personalised messages always out performs generic messaging
- The majority of our users are interested in saving
