project context
Savvy is a startup setup to address the needs of commercial property managers and tennants. Savvy identified the challenge for property managers to maximise properities rentable space, while providing sufficient working space for tenants. Savvy Property is a Proof of concept app tackling this challenge by giving property managers accurate and thorough insight of their properties occupancy.
core functions
Feed - Users can see occupancy updates and insights of their properties.
sperties - Users can access all their properties occupancy data.
Occupancy - Users can compare the occupancy data of their properties.
