CV

I’m a product designer working in London, with over 4 years experience. Prior to this, I studied and worked in architecture, gaining a masters and working in practice for 3 years. While my career has changed, my passion and philosophy for design has not. I’m motivated by creating useful products that people look forward to using, and improve their lives. In my spare time, I can be found on a basketball court, a rugby pitch or on the sofa watching comedy.

Experience

Product design

ClearScore

10/2018 - Today

ClearScore’s an established Fintech startup, with offices in London, South Africa and Australia. The ClearScore app is one of the most widely known credit monitoring apps in the uk and has over 10 million users. Working at largely design-led company, allowed me to take ownership of the design direction and noticeably improve the product in my time there. ClearScore work with a squad structure, where each squad is responsible for a specific strategic objective. I took the opportunity to work in a variety of squads to give me exposure to different design problems and business objectives. For example, working in squads linked closely with ClearScores marketplace presented challenges balancing user and business goals. Whereas, working in a squad focused on user activation and engagement solved problems of how to provide significant continued user value. Often working as the sole designer on projects, allowed me to take full ownership of projects and provide all necessary design deliverables. Working largely in the solution space, I’m very comfortable taking proven user problem through to final release.
